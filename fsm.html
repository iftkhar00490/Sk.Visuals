What is Food-Vision ?

Food-Vision is an innovative app that leverages state-of-the-art image recognition technology to identify a wide range of food items from photos. The app's extensive database includes a variety of popular foods, meats, and desserts, among others.

Its a cutting-edge app that offers many benefits to users. Its accuracy and speed in identifying food items, combined with its user-friendly interface, make it a valuable tool for both individuals and businesses.
